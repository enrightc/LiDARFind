ABOUT LiDARFind

Discover and share archaeological finds through our unique LiDAR data viewing platform, dedicated to uncovering the hidden historical treasures of Wales. LiDARFind allows you to search and explore Welsh LiDAR data, revealing archaeological sites that have remained hidden for centuries.

Join our vibrant community of enthusiasts and professionals who are passionate about Welsh heritage. By contributing to the exploration and documentation of these historical sites, you will help to expand our collective knowledge of Wales' rich and diverse history.

Our user-friendly interface ensures that both novices and experts can easily navigate and utilise the platform to uncover the past and enrich our understanding of Welsh heritage. Whether you're passionate about history or a professional archaeologist, LiDARFind offers you the tools to make meaningful discoveries about Wales' unique historic environment.

Start exploring today and become a part of a community dedicated to discovering and understanding the extraordinary heritage of Wales.
